## Status definitions

- **Stands** — survives counterevidence as written; no material change.
- **Qualified** — survives in substance/direction; requires explicit scope, conditionality, or caveat.
- **Corrected** — a specific figure, source attribution, or framing is wrong and must change.
- **New** — a claim or caveat introduced by Stage 4; must be added going forward.
