## Notes

Full article now accessible. Implements and validates a practical traceability system (feature-based and continuous marking) at electrode production scale, demonstrating how electrode-sheet-level data allocation can enable virtual quality gates and sigma-unit adjustment. Documents critical traceability points (CTPs) where systematic loss of information occurs and shows how skilled data-driven teams manage complexity across converging/diverging process flows. The framework emphasizes human-intensive knowledge work: situation analysis, identification of relevant data points, and interpretation of quality data for process decisions. Demonstrates that while automation (markings, imaging) plays a role, the workforce demand shifts from reactive inspection to proactive data interpretation, process optimization, and virtual quality gate management—roles requiring technical depth in process control, statistics, and systems thinking.
