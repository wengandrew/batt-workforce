## Extracted Quotes / Claims
> "A geographical region that roughly runs between Michigan to Tennessee to Georgia to western New York" defines the Battery Belt, with gigafactories clustering in Midwest/South plus California and Texas.

> "Since early 2021, over 15 new battery gigafactories or expansions have been announced, representing a potential investment of at least $40 billion."

> "US battery capacity is projected to grow more than fivefold from 2021 to 2026 and expand an additional 86% by 2031."

> "Ford: $50 billion through 2026 for EV expansion; GM: $35 billion through 2025, plus a $2.5 billion DOE loan with LG for three gigafactories creating 11,100 jobs."
