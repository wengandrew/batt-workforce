## Extracted Quotes / Claims

> "L-H Battery Company, a joint venture between LG Energy Solution and Honda, committed $3.5 billion to build an EV battery plant in Jeffersonville, Ohio, with an additional $700 million from Honda for retooling existing facilities."

> "Following the February 2023 groundbreaking, LGES-Honda had hired over 600 associates by March 2026, including engineers, machine operators, maintenance technicians, and managers."

> "The organization coordinated with state partners including the Greater Ohio Workforce Board, Ohio Department of Job and Family Services, Governor's Office of Workforce Transformation, and Ohio Association of Community Colleges."

> "Opening an OhioMeansJobs recruitment center near the facility; Creating and distributing a hiring guide to 200+ educational institutions and community organizations; Hosting webinars attended by nearly 100 partners"
