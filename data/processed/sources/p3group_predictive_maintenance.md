## Extracted Quotes / Claims

> "The need for reduced downtime and maintenance expenses is a critical aspect for manufacturers in a competitive market."

> "For maintenance roles, it is important to get mechanical as well as predictive understanding, leading to predictive maintenance, contamination control, etc. Additional support roles like applications engineers, field service engineers, and others, bridge the knowledge gap between the floor and the technology. This can especially help when training workers for new chemistry, operational upskilling, and most importantly cross-functional troubleshooting."

> "The increase in battery production including numerous gigafactories implies inevitable improvement in manufacturing efficiency and productivity. Due to this rapid development, integrated engineering systems become increasingly complex. This entails unanticipated faults in production processes that range from simple replacements to accidents, provoking fast costs in lost productivity as well as human and environmental resources." (p. 2, Master Thesis)

> "Organizing resources (e.g. service technicians)" and "Production and maintenance schedule optimization" as key benefits of predictive maintenance implementation (p. 5, Master Thesis)

> "Expert domain knowledge for equipment modelling" identified as a critical prerequisite for predictive maintenance strategy implementation (p. 13, Master Thesis)
