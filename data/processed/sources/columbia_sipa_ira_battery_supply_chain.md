## Extracted Quotes / Claims

> "Approximately 80% of clean-tech and semiconductor investments announced in Republican-leaning states. Major concentrations in South Carolina, Georgia, Michigan, and Ohio."

> "Over $110 billion in clean energy investments followed the IRA's passage; approximately $70 billion earmarked specifically for US battery supply chains. US gigafactory capacity in pipeline increased from ~700 GWh (July 2022) to 1.2 TWh (July 2023)."

> "US production costs: $135/kWh for NCM 811 cells; $125/kWh for LFP cells. China's capex intensity: ~$60 million/GWh vs. US average of ~$90 million/GWh. IRA tax credits reduce costs by approximately $45/kWh."
