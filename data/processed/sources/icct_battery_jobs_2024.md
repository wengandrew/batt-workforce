## Extracted Quotes / Claims

> "Battery cell, module, and pack production: Facilities need electrical, electronic, and electromechanical specialists who assemble battery cells, modules, and packs using automation equipment or manual tools. These jobs usually require a high school diploma or equivalent. Median salaries for these jobs range from approximately $39,000 to $50,000 annually in 2023."

> "Integrated cell, module, and pack manufacturing is the most labor-intensive segment. Median employment is roughly 90 jobs per GWh, with an average near 95 jobs per GWh. Estimates vary widely, ranging from about 60 to 165 jobs per GWh."

> "Module and pack production alone requires fewer workers. Median employment is approximately 50 jobs per GWh. Reported estimates range from about 35 to 70 jobs per GWh."

> "Cathode production is the most labor-intensive battery component manufacturing segment. Median employment is roughly 15 jobs per GWh, with estimates ranging from 10 to 24 jobs per GWh."

> "Battery recycling is among the most labor-intensive activities in the battery supply chain. Median employment is approximately 19 jobs per GWh. Estimates range widely from about 6 to 27 jobs per GWh."
