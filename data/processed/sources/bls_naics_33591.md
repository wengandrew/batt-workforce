## Notes

**Scope and limitations:**
- NAICS 33591 is the official battery manufacturing classification, broadest definition
- Includes non-Li-ion chemistry (lead-acid, solid-state precursors) and some charger manufacturing, not purely Li-ion cell focus
- May 2026 figure is seasonally adjusted preliminary estimate
- This is the official US headcount benchmark for all battery manufacturing per BLS

**Cross-references:**
