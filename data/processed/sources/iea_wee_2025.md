## Notes

**Scope and significance:**
- IEA's 2025 world energy employment report
- Includes combined EV + battery employment figures
- Critical limitation: no GWh denominator, making per-unit intensity calculations impossible
- Global scope covering all regions and value-chain segments

**Data limitations:**
- Combined EV + battery employment makes isolation of battery sector difficult
- No segment breakdown (mining, refining, cell, module, pack, assembly)
- Global aggregate masks regional variation in labor intensity (China ~50 vs US ~315 per cru_ritchie_china_intensity)
- Useful for overall employment scale-check but not for sensitivity modeling

**Cross-references:**
- Compare to iea_gevo_2025 which provides GWh demand projections (enabling denominator-based analysis)
- Compare to upjohn_naatbatt_2023 US value-chain 318.5 jobs/GWh baseline for regional benchmark
