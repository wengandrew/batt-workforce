## Extracted Quotes / Claims

> "We see that workforce demand remains substantial overall, though the challenge is a shift from lower skill, repetitive, traditional production-line type roles (replaced by automation) to higher skilled roles. This is also where we see the largest gap in the workforce (skilled trade or journeyman level roles - maintenance, controls technician, manufacturing tech, production tech - increasingly more demand with a depleting workforce). State level workforce development programs in the US are starting to catch up with this need, and we are seeing more grant funded training and apprenticeship programs working to address this gap in specialized and skilled advanced manufacturing labor pool."
