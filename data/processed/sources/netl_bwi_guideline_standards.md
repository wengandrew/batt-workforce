## Notes

This is an authoritative federal source establishing standardized competency frameworks for two critical battery manufacturing roles. The structured apprenticeship pathway (8,000 hours plus 608 hours classroom) and technical skill requirements provide granular insight into workforce composition and training intensity. Useful for understanding skill gaps and training duration assumptions in productivity models. No quantitative employment data in this source itself, but directly informs workforce taxonomy and training cost calculations.
