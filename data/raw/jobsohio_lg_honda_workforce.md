# LG Energy Solution-Honda Workforce Success Story

**Project Overview**
L-H Battery Company, a joint venture between LG Energy Solution and Honda, committed $3.5 billion to build an EV battery plant in Jeffersonville, Ohio, with an additional $700 million from Honda for retooling existing facilities.

**Challenge**
The partnership needed a comprehensive workforce strategy to support scaling EV battery production and attract technical talent to meet critical skill gaps.

**JobsOhio's Response**
The organization coordinated with state partners including the Greater Ohio Workforce Board, Ohio Department of Job and Family Services, Governor's Office of Workforce Transformation, and Ohio Association of Community Colleges. Key initiatives included:

- Opening an OhioMeansJobs recruitment center near the facility
- Creating and distributing a hiring guide to 200+ educational institutions and community organizations
- Hosting webinars attended by nearly 100 partners

**Results**
Following the February 2023 groundbreaking, LGES-Honda had hired over 600 associates by March 2026, including engineers, machine operators, maintenance technicians, and managers.

**Employee Success Example**
Anthony Harrington joined as an equipment service technician in 2024 and earned two promotions within 18 months, advancing to equipment service planner overseeing production equipment maintenance and reliability.

**Company Perspective**
According to HR Lead Craig Fultz: "Team Ohio has been an invaluable partner in helping us engage with Ohio's workforce."
