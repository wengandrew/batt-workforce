# Kentucky News: BlueOval SK Battery Park Construction Advances

**Key Developments**

Governor Andy Beshear toured the BlueOval SK Battery Park in Glendale on August 9, 2023, as construction progresses on the second battery manufacturing facility. The joint venture between Ford and SK On has invested $5.8 billion in the project.

**Construction Progress**

The two battery plants, each measuring approximately 4 million square feet, are on schedule. Recent accomplishments include:

- Moving 6.8 million cubic yards of earth
- Placing 257,000 cubic yards of concrete
- Erecting 44,000 tons of steel
- Installing 2.8 million square feet of roofing
- Installing 535,000 square feet of exterior siding

The facilities will collectively produce over 80 gigawatt hours annually for electric vehicle batteries.

**Employment and Training**

Nearly 2,600 construction workers are currently on-site. The project will create 5,000 permanent jobs, with hiring for hourly positions already underway. The $25 million ECTC BlueOval SK Training Center, scheduled for completion in 2024, will prepare workers through virtual reality labs and industrial maintenance training.

**Community Impact**

Ford has invested nearly $40,000 in Glendale improvements, including park refreshes, sidewalk repairs, handicap-accessible ramps, and fire department equipment. The project contributes to Kentucky's growing EV sector, representing $10.9 billion in announced investment statewide.
