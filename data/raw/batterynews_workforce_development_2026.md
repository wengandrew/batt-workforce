# Battery Training and Workforce Development in North America

## Key Findings

The article highlights that "North America is continuously expanding its capacity in the battery industry." As new cell factories and recycling plants come online, employers increasingly need qualified technical personnel, engineers, and production managers.

## Training Infrastructure

Rather than relying on standalone programs, North American initiatives are organized through several institutional channels:

- National research laboratories
- Industry associations
- Universities and colleges
- Regionally funded training programs

## Approach to Skill Development

The programs are strategically designed to align with industrial expansion. They are "directly linked to the construction and scaling of gigafactories as well as recycling sites" and emphasize rapid on-site skills development.

## Data Source

Battery-News compiled this information in collaboration with the Volta Foundation for their 2025 Battery Report, with updates provided for this article. The outlet invites readers to report missing training programs or provide feedback through their contact channels.
