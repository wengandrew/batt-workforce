Battery Manufacturing Labor Intensity

How many workers each battery plant runs per GWh of yearly output, and how that ratio moves as plants scale. Open a site to see its headcount, output, and labor-intensity detail. The last tab lines up all four sites on one common (“normalized”) definition. Every point carries its own source, shown when you select the point.

Quality tags: PRIMARY filing/audited · SECONDARY press · DERIVED computed from sources · ESTIMATE needs an assumption
Confidence: ● High · ● Med · ● Low
Attribute chips on each series show the denominator basis and what the headcount covers (CELL / PACK / UPSTREAM / CONSTR).

Every value lives in battman_data.js (single source of truth) and is drawn here by battman_shared.js. Keep the three files together in this folder. Ratios, derived produced-GWh figures, utilization estimates and fitted curves are analyst computations from the sourced inputs and are labeled as such. Fit parameters are computed numerically (SciPy) on exactly the plotted points. Annotations work offline; source links require internet.
